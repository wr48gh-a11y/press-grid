Welcome to Press Grid

Turn a local business story into a board of synthetic media scout cards, each showing who might care, what angle to lead with, and how to pitch it.

  1. 1 Frame the story Set the business, market, and goal.
  2. 2 Generate the board Create eight scout cards, one for each media lane.
  3. 3 Choose the pitch Flip the strongest cards for angle, proof, and outreach guidance.

Demo only. Every reporter, outlet, and pitch detail is synthetic. No real journalists, scraping, or email.
